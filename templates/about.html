About This Project

Multi-Regime Market Detection Engine

Project Overview

The Multi-Regime Market Detection Engine is an automated system that detects and classifies financial market conditions in real-time, providing traders and investors with actionable insights for better decision-making.

Financial markets are inherently non-stationary and constantly shift between different regimes. Manual detection of these shifts is slow, subjective, and often leads to delayed reactions and poor trading decisions. Our system solves this problem by combining machine learning, data science, and cloud technology to automatically detect market regimes and provide strategy recommendations.

Our Approach: Two Core Components

Machine Learning Engine (DSBDA)

Analyzes market data using K-Means clustering to automatically detect and classify market regimes (Bull, Bear, Sideways, Volatile) with dynamic confidence scores. Updates every 5 minutes with fresh data.

  • Algorithm: K-Means Clustering (4 regimes)
  • Data Source: Yahoo Finance (yfinance) with synthetic fallback
  • Features: 7 engineered technical indicators (Log Returns, Volatility, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR)
  • Real-time Updates: APScheduler background job every 5 minutes
Web Application (WAD)

Interactive, real-time dashboard displaying current market regime, confidence levels, and regime transitions. Frontend continuously polls backend every 10 seconds for the latest regime classification.

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, Vanilla JavaScript
  • Backend: Flask 2.3.3 REST API
  • Visualization: Chart.js 3.9.1 for interactive charts
  • Live Updates: 10-second polling interval with real-time data fetch

Understanding Market Regimes

Bull Market

Characterized by rising prices and strong upward momentum. Markets trend higher with increased investor confidence and buying pressure.

Characteristics:

  • Rising price trends
  • High confidence
  • Strong volumes
  • Lower volatility
Bear Market

Characterized by falling prices and strong downward momentum. Markets trend lower with decreased investor confidence and selling pressure.

Characteristics:

  • Falling price trends
  • Low sentiment
  • Selling pressure
  • Unpredictable moves
Sideways Market

Characterized by low momentum and range-bound movement. Prices consolidate between support and resistance levels without clear directional bias.

Characteristics:

  • Consolidation phase
  • Low trend strength
  • Mean reversion
  • Predictable ranges
High Volatility

Characterized by extreme price swings and high uncertainty. Markets experience rapid, unpredictable movements in both directions with increased risk.

Characteristics:

  • Extreme moves
  • Uncertainty
  • Wide ranges
  • High risk
