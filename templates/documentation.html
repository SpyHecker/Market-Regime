How To Use MRDE

The Multi-Regime Market Detection Engine analyzes live market data and classifies the market into distinct "Regimes" so you can trade with clarity.

⚡

1. The Dashboard

Your main hub. Here you'll see the Active Market Regime updated in real-time, along with current volatility (VIX) and our Machine Learning Model's confidence score.

📈

2. Historical Analysis

Use the Analysis tools to dive into the past. Filter by 1 Month, 1 Year, or 5 Years to see exactly how long previous bull or bear markets lasted.

🧠

3. ML Insights

Curious how the algorithm thinks? The ML Insights page shows the raw mathematical features (like RSI and MACD) driving the current classification.

Understanding The Regimes

Bull Market

A fast-moving upward trend. Characterized by high investor confidence, low relative volatility, and consistent green day-over-day returns.

STRATEGY: Favorable for long positions and momentum trades.

Bear Market

A downward trend marked by investor fear, high volatility spikes, and unpredictable pullbacks. Risk is generally elevated.

STRATEGY: Short positions, defensive plays, tight stop-losses.

Sideways / Neutral

The market is chopping up and down without a clear long-term direction. VIX is usually subdued but prices fail to break out.

STRATEGY: Mean-reversion trading, option-selling strategies.
