The Problem

Markets Don't Stay the Same — But Most Tools Do

A strategy built for a bull market destroys capital in a bear market. A momentum trade in a sideways market bleeds you dry on fees. Most platforms show you price — but not what the market is actually doing.

MRDE solves this by continuously classifying the underlying market regime, so your strategy posture matches the environment — not just your hopes.

📈
Bull Regime
Sustained uptrend with compressing volatility. Trend-following and momentum strategies perform best here.
📉
Bear Regime
Downtrend driven by macro stress or sector rotation. Capital preservation and short exposure take priority.
➡️
Sideways Regime
No directional edge. Mean-reversion, range trading, and options theta strategies thrive in range-bound markets.
⚡
High-Volatility Regime
Explosive, unpredictable moves. Oversized positions become liabilities. Hedging and sizing discipline are critical.

Under the Hood

Rigorous Data. Production-Grade Models.

MRDE is built on a principled data pipeline and a dual-model architecture that combines unsupervised clustering with probabilistic state modelling.

Market data is ingested from multiple asset classes via yfinance, covering equities, ETFs, and indices. Features are derived from log returns, rolling volatility, trend signals, and cross-asset spreads.

Regime classification runs through two complementary models: K-Means clustering provides fast, hard-boundary regime labels calibrated on historical distributions. Hidden Markov Models (HMM) layer in temporal state transitions — modelling regime persistence, switching probabilities, and forward-looking confidence scores.
